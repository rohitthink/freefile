## Full Description

FreeFile ITR is a completely free, privacy-first income tax return filing app built specifically for Indian freelancers, consultants, and independent professionals.

KEY FEATURES:

Bank Statement Import
Upload bank statements from HDFC, SBI, ICICI, Axis, and Kotak banks. Transactions are automatically categorized into income and expense categories.

Smart Tax Computation
Supports both ITR-4 (presumptive taxation under 44ADA) and ITR-3 (regular business income). Automatically computes tax under both old and new regimes and recommends the one that saves you more.

Deduction Tracking
Track all your deductions under Section 80C, 80D, 80CCD(1B), 80E, 80G, 80TTA, and more. See how much you save with each deduction.

TDS Credit Management
Import TDS credits from Form 26AS or add them manually. Track deductor details, TAN numbers, and amounts.

Advance Tax Schedule
Know exactly how much advance tax to pay and when, with quarterly installment breakdowns.

Old vs New Regime Comparison
Side-by-side comparison of your tax liability under both regimes with a clear recommendation on which saves you more money.

PDF Reports
Generate a professional ITR summary PDF with all your income, deductions, and tax computation details.

PRIVACY FIRST:
All your financial data stays on your device. Bank statements are processed securely and never stored on any server. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

SUPPORTED:
- Banks: HDFC, SBI, ICICI, Axis, Kotak (PDF and CSV)
- ITR Forms: ITR-3 and ITR-4
- Tax Regimes: Old and New (FY 2025-26)
- Deductions: All major Chapter VI-A sections

Built by freelancers, for freelancers.
