# FreeFile ITR - Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 26, 2026

FreeFile ITR ("the App") is a free, local-first income tax return filing application for Indian freelancers. Your privacy is fundamental to how we built this app.

## Data Storage

All your financial data — transactions, income details, deductions, TDS entries, and profile information — is stored **locally on your device only**. We do not operate any database server that stores your personal or financial data.

## PDF Processing

When you upload a bank statement or Form 26AS PDF for parsing, the file is sent to our cloud processing service solely to extract transaction data. The processing happens in real-time and:

- Your PDF files are **not stored** on our servers
- Extracted data is returned to your device and stored locally
- No copies of your documents are retained after processing
- Our processing servers do not log file contents

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- No personal identification information is transmitted to any server
- No PAN, Aadhaar, or financial account numbers leave your device
- No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking
- No advertising identifiers or third-party SDKs
- No cookies or web tracking
- No location data

## ITR Filing Automation

The filing automation feature opens a browser window directly to the official Income Tax Department portal (incometax.gov.in). Your login credentials (password and OTP) are entered by you directly in the portal — they are never captured, stored, or transmitted by the App.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. Period.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored on your device, you have full control. Uninstalling the App removes all data. You can also delete your data from within the App's settings at any time.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
